Our Inspiration

In Loving Memory
 John Kitkowski
12/15/1997 - 12/23/2017

John,

You believed in us before the logos, before the releases, before we even knew our own potential. 2018 and beyond is for you.

Your presence was not coincidence.

I was not your brother,
but you treated me so.

We will carry on everything you left us with. Your mind was as big as the universe and you taught me the importance of perseverance and mental strength.
We will keep the wheel spinning for you.
All of us

-Isaiah

John,

As this mission continues, you are our biggest inspiration. We will become what you always said we would. The years ahead are for you.

You shined even in the darkest of light.

You believed in us.
You were family.

You had a bright future ahead of you, and we were so excited to see what the future had in store for us. Please stay with us from above and guide us through this journey. We need you.
Our motivation is through the roof. We will do this for you.
Rest easy brother.

-Isaac

JOHN MICHAEL KITKOWSKI BELOVED SON, BROTHER, FRIEND
